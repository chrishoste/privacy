# Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 5, 2026

EV kWh Tracker is a simple app for recording electric vehicle charging sessions.

## Data Collected

The app lets you enter charging information such as:

- charging amount in kWh
- charging cost
- charging date
- charging location type
- car name

This information is entered by you and is used only to provide the app's charging history and summary features.

## Data Storage

Your app data is stored on your device using Apple's local persistence frameworks.

If iCloud is enabled for the app and your device, your app data may sync through Apple's iCloud and CloudKit services so it is available on your devices signed in with the same Apple ID.

## Tracking and Analytics

EV kWh Tracker does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies.

## Data Sharing

EV kWh Tracker does not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

Data synced through iCloud is handled by Apple according to Apple's iCloud terms and privacy practices.

## Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page.

## Contact

For privacy-related questions, please use the support or contact option provided on the app's App Store product page.
