# EV kWh Tracker Support

Thank you for using EV kWh Tracker.

## Getting Help

If you need help with the app, please use the support or contact option provided on the app's App Store product page.

## Common Questions

### Where is my data stored?

Your charging data is stored on your device. If iCloud is enabled for the app and your device, your data may sync through Apple's iCloud and CloudKit services.

### Does the app require an account?

No. EV kWh Tracker does not require a separate account.

### Does the app track me?

No. EV kWh Tracker does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies.

### How can I reset my data?

The app includes a clear data option in the debug area of settings. This is intended for private demo and testing use.
